Discovering insights with SQL: Investigating Logistics Company Dataset

The logistics company operates in both international and domestic sectors, focusing on cost-efficient integration of suppliers, manufacturers, and import/export processes. Customers can send various shipping content, with payment made upon delivery, and shipment status updates handled by delivery personnel and center heads. Effective product delivery, supported by infrastructure like transport and storage, plays a key role in driving the growth of both domestic and international trade.

Objectives:
✪ Design and implement a comprehensive logistics management database schema focused on optimizing cost efficiency and enhancing customer satisfaction. This system will streamline operational integration by connecting key activities such as suppliers, manufacturers, and import/export processes across domestic and international logistics.
✪ By enabling data-driven decision-making and performance tracking, the database will improve the speed and reliability of logistics services. This will allow for more efficient resource management, ensuring faster deliveries and better overall service quality.
